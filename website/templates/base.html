    Mortality

    Cancer is one of the most prevalent cause of mortality globally, responsible for almost ten million deaths each year.

    Demography

    In Nigeria, cancer is responsible for 70,000 deaths annually (28 414 for male and 41 913 for female).Breast cancer is Nigeria's most diagnosed cancer

    Diagnosis

    Diagnosis of breast cancer can be performed by breast examination, mammograms, breast ultrasound and biopsy which involves removing a sample of breast cells for testing.

    Risk Factors

    The fundamental factors that determines breast cancer survivability are proper risk assessment, as well as early and accurate diagnosis.
