About Medicio

Medicio is a breast cancer prediction and diagnosis system developed to automate the diagnosis of breast cancer in patients using artificial intelligence techniques.

Features of Medicio Breast Cancer System

Medicio breast cancer diagnostic system posesses a variety of unique features among which include:

  • Automation of breast cancer diagnosis system
  • Fast , quick and powerful algorithms (ensemble)
  • Simple to learn and easy to use

Medicio system caters to radiographers as well end user patients who seek quick and easy diagnosis of breast cancer. With a few clicks, users can diagnose breast cancer hence this system can act as a second opinion to doctors , oncologists or in times of emergency
